## Description

The `requirements` exporter shall enable Excel auto-filter on the Requirements sheet.

## Rationale

Auto-filter allows reviewers to narrow the requirements list by type, folder, or verification method without exporting a separate filtered view.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The Requirements sheet has auto-filter enabled on the header row.
